About Me

My research focuses on the resilience of structural systems against natural disasters, particularly in the context of earthquake damage assessment and progressive collapse prevention. Under the mentorship of Professor Shuang Li, I am utilizing cutting-edge techniques, such as artificial intelligence and machine learning, to enhance structural safety and predict the performance of buildings during and after seismic events. Additionally, I am exploring innovative approaches for ensuring the long-term resilience and sustainability of infrastructure in disaster-prone areas.
